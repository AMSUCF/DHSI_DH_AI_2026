## Coding AI Interfaces with Claude

<p class="page-lead" markdown="1">Move from a rule-based system to an <em>AI-powered</em> one. Instead of fixed logic, your application will interpret what the user types and generate a personalized response—built and refined entirely by prompt.</p>

<div class="accordion-content" markdown="1">

Start from the recommender concept from the [previous demo](distant-coding.html)—but this time, build it as an **AI app**. Begin your prompt by saying you want to build "an AI app," which signals Claude to use its AI-powered Artifact features rather than fixed, rule-based logic.

Refine through **at least five more prompts** to make the app your own and distinct from any sample. Don't just polish—you might try something totally different in approach or interface.

Ask for **new designs and interface concepts**, and describe the features you want **as concretely as possible**. Specificity is what separates a generic result from one that fits your idea.
